Privacy Policy

At Image Converter, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our Image Converter tool.

If you have any concerns about the security of your personal information, please contact us using the information provided below.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
